How to Use This Chatbot
Welcome to our website! This chatbot is here to assist you in making homemade Filipino recipes. Follow these simple steps to make the most out of your interaction:
1. Start a Conversation
- Use our suggested messages to get the conversation started.
- From using this feature, you can already pick a Filipino recipe to cook with.
- You can also chat your own questions but make it strictly about Filipino cuisine.
2. Be Specific with Your Queries
- For better results, ask clear and specific questions. For example:
"Can you explain what this recipe is about?"
"What ingredients are used?"
"How do I make this recipe?"
3. Use keywords for faster assistance.
- Use keywords like:
"Help"
"Instructions"
"Details about breakfast Filipino recipes"
4. Feedback and Suggestions
- If the chatbot cannot answer your question, let it know by typing, "I need help with something else."
- Your feedback is valuable, and we'll work on improving the responses. You can do that by contacting us in the suggested email addresses of each member.
5. Stay Curious!
- Don't hesitate to explore or experiment with your queries. The chatbot is here to learn and help!
Enjoy your visit and let us know if there's anything else you need!
